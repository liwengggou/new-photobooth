## LIGHTING SPECIFICATIONS (PRIMARY TRANSFORMATION)

### Main Key Light
```
Position: Slightly above eye level, frontal (10-15° above)
Type: Large softbox or diffused panel (simulating 80cm+ source)
Color temperature: 5400K (neutral daylight)
Intensity: Bright but not blown out
Quality: SOFT - very diffused edges
Distance: Close enough for wrap-around effect
```

### Fill Light
```
Position: Frontal, at eye level
Type: Reflector or secondary soft panel
Color temperature: 5400K (matching key)
Intensity: 60-70% of key light
Purpose: Lift shadows without eliminating them
Ratio: 1.5:1 to 2:1 (key to fill)
```

### Resulting Light Behavior
```
Shadows: Present but SOFT - gradual falloff, not harsh edges
Contrast: LOW to MEDIUM - no dramatic chiaroscuro
Highlights: CONTROLLED - no hot spots or blown areas
Wrap: Light wraps around face contours gently
Uniformity: Even illumination across facial planes
```

### Catch Lights
```
Shape: Soft rectangular (from softbox)
Position: Upper portion of iris
Size: Moderate, natural looking
Count: 1-2 per eye (not multiple ring light dots)
```

## SKIN APPEARANCE (LIGHT-ACHIEVED, NOT FILTERED)

### What Good Lighting Does:
- **Reduces** visible micro-contrast from pores (but doesn't eliminate)
- **Softens** shadow transitions on skin surface
- **Evens** apparent tone through uniform illumination
- **Minimizes** harsh texture shadows
- **Preserves** natural skin character

### Skin Quality Parameters:
| Aspect | Treatment |
|--------|-----------|
| Pores | **VISIBLE** but shadow-softened |
| Texture | **NATURAL** - real skin appearance |
| Blemishes | Softened by lighting, not removed |
| Tone evenness | Improved through light distribution |
| Smoothing filter | **NONE** - absolutely forbidden |
| Blur effect | **NONE** - no soft focus filter |
| Plastic appearance | **FORBIDDEN** |

### The Natural Glow Effect:
Korean photo booths achieve "glowing" skin through:
- High-key lighting reducing shadow depth
- Soft fill eliminating under-eye darkness
- Even exposure preventing patchy appearance
- NOT through artificial smoothing
